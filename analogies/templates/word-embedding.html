What the hell is a word embedding?

In a nutshell, a word embedding is a vector representation of a word that reflects its meaning.
Words that are related (dolphin, whale, shark, octopus) have embeddings which are close together.
Embeddings are so powerful that one can even use them to solve analogies

vector("woman") + vector("king") - vector("man") = ~ vector("queen") {% endblock %}
